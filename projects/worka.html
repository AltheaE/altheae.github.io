Worka

Design and implementation of web and portfolio templates

The client

Worka is an online portfolio platform with different templates to choose from.

The project

The project consist of a series of new templates, a website and research about UX patterns and general improvement of user dashboard.

One of the main goals to differentiate itself from other portfolio competitors was the focus on mobile view and responsiveness of the web.
